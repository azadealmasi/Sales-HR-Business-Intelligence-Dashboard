# Sales & HR Business Intelligence Dashboard

This project presents an end-to-end **Business Intelligence and data analytics solution** built using **Power BI**, designed to transform raw sales and HR data into **clear, actionable insights** for decision-makers.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly understand **revenue performance, product effectiveness, regional trends, and workforce structure**, supporting strategic planning across sales and human resources.

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyse overall business performance using key sales and HR metrics  
- Identify **top- and bottom-performing products**
- Understand **revenue distribution across countries**
- Provide insight into **employee salary structure, retention, and demographics**
- Deliver an **interactive, executive-ready dashboard** for fast decision-making

---

## Dataset Overview

The dataset contains aggregated **Sales** and **Human Resources** data, including:

### Sales Data
- Product categories and individual products  
- Sales quantity and total revenue  
- Revenue by country and year  

### HR Data
- Number of employees  
- Gender distribution  
- Salary metrics (min, average, max)  
- Employee retention status  
- Salary distribution by department
